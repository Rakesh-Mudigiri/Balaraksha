# 🛡️ Balaraksha – Child Welfare & Rescue Platform

## Overview

Balaraksha is a technology-driven child welfare platform designed to connect vulnerable, orphaned, and abandoned children with verified shelters, volunteers, and government authorities. The platform streamlines rescue, rehabilitation, and family reunification through real-time reporting, shelter coordination, and case tracking.

The goal of Balaraksha is to create a safer and more responsive child protection ecosystem by leveraging technology to improve communication, coordination, and accessibility among all stakeholders.

---

## Problem Statement

Many child welfare processes rely on fragmented communication and manual coordination, leading to delays in rescue and rehabilitation efforts.

Balaraksha addresses these challenges by providing a centralized digital platform that enables faster reporting, efficient shelter allocation, and transparent case management.
