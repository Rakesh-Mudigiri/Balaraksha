Our Mission

Technology-Driven Child Protection

We're building a comprehensive ecosystem that ensures no child in Telangana remains without proper care and support.

🚨

Instant Reporting

Report a child in need with photos, location, and details. Anonymous reporting available for sensitive situations.

🎯

Smart Matching

AI-powered algorithm matches children to the nearest suitable shelter based on capacity, specialization, and needs.

🏛️

Government Integration

Seamless integration with DCPU, Police, JJ Board, and other government departments for official case management.

📊

Complete Tracking

End-to-end case tracking from reporting through rehabilitation, family reunification, or successful placement.

💚

Holistic Care

Medical care, education, counseling, and skill development programs for complete child rehabilitation.

🔒

Secure & Compliant

Built with JJ Act, POCSO, and data protection compliance. Child data is encrypted and protected.

Simple Process

How It Works

Our streamlined process ensures rapid response and proper care for every child.

1
📱

Report

Citizen reports a child in need via app or call. Location, photos, and description captured automatically.

2
🔍

Verify & Match

System verifies report, assesses urgency, and finds the nearest appropriate shelter with available capacity.

3
🚗

Dispatch & Rescue

Volunteers and authorities dispatched. Child is safely transported to the matched shelter facility.

4
🏥

Immediate Care

Medical checkup, basic needs assessment, trauma counseling, and safe accommodation provided immediately.

5
⚖️

Legal Process

JJ Board case filing, court proceedings, family investigation, and legal documentation handled systematically.

6
🎓

Rehabilitation

Education enrollment, skill development, long-term care, and eventual family reunification or adoption.
