From the Streets to a Loving Home: Lakshmi's Journey

Found alone at Secunderabad railway station at age 4, Lakshmi's life changed when a citizen reported her through Balaraksha. Within hours, she was rescued and placed in a safe shelter. After 3 months of investigation, her family was located in Karimnagar. Today, she's back home, attending school, and dreaming of becoming a teacher.
